About Date Buddy

Date Buddy is a Hyderabad-based social companionship platform designed to help people connect for meaningful, real-world experiences. Whether it's grabbing a coffee, watching a movie, shopping, or attending an event, Date Buddy makes it easier to find a compatible companion for public social outings.

We believe everyone deserves connection, conversation, and shared experiences in a safe, respectful, and transparent environment.
